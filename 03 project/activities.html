Universities are usually organized into colleges or schools, such as a college of arts and sciences, a business school, a law school, or a medical school. In many countries, universities are either public or private institutions, and they are often funded by government subsidies, tuition fees, and private donations. Admission to a university usually requires a high school diploma or equivalent, standardized test scores, and other application materials.
